About our Website

Encrypted File Vault is a secure and user-friendly web application designed to encrypt and decrypt your text files using powerful cryptographic algorithms. Whether for personal, academic, or professional use, our platform ensures your data remains private and protected.

üöÄ How to Use

  1. Register: Create an account with your name, email, and password.
  2. Log In: Sign in with your credentials. A One-Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your email.
  3. Enter the OTP: Complete the login process securely.
  4. Upload a Text File: Choose the file you wish to encrypt or decrypt.
  5. Choose an Operation:
    • Encrypt: File will be securely encrypted and stored.
    • Decrypt: Decrypt previously encrypted files with valid credentials.
  6. Download the Result: Optionally upload to linked cloud storage.
